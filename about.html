So, who are you people?

Right now, we're a bunch of programmers, modellers, rippers, and other junk from all across the planet. We'll be famous one day, but for now, we code our asses off for this Gmod addon (which is pretty expansive at this point).

Our team consists of Hoffa, StarChick, Killst3aks, aftokinito, and various community contributions. dane00a currently maintains and updates the website.

What exactly IS Neurotec?

Ok, look, if you didn't catch it on any of the other pages, this is a modification/addon compilation for Garry's Mod, a sandbox physics game. It doesn't require any external dependencies such as Counter Strike: Source and etc.

Why Isn't This on the Workshop?

Simple: This addon is ginormous; it's over 10 gigabytes. Since the Workshop only allows uploads of 100mB per addon, we'd need about 100 parts, give or take, and it's too difficult to maintain those parts.
